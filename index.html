Belly Button Biodiversity Dashboard

Use the interactive charts below to explore the dataset

This project does a research on different species of bacteria which can be found on the human body. Different samples have been collected from a number of participants as well as participant information like age, ethnicity etc. With this webpage, participants who took part in the study can with their IDs find out which bacteria species live on their bodies.
